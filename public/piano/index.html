Piano Glide

Coming next

Piano practice on macOS with falling notes, MIDI input, and a built-in library to keep the session moving.

Piano Glide is an upcoming macOS app focused on practice flow: connect a MIDI keyboard, load a song, and follow the music with a responsive falling-note view while tracking timing and streaks.

It is not in the store yet. This page is an early preview of what is coming next from Cuatro Studio.

What Piano Glide is built for

  • Practice piano pieces with a visual falling-note playback view
  • Play with a connected MIDI keyboard on macOS
  • Learn from a built-in song library and import MIDI songs
  • Track performance with timing, streak, and progress feedback
