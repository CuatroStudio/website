Week Number

New release: v1.0

Which week is it? Settled.

This lightweight macOS app gives you three clear, preset widgets—no configuration, no fuss—so the week number is always in view.

What’s inside

  • Month Calendar — Shows the current month and highlights the current week and today for instant context.
  • Big Week + Progress — Displays the current week number in a large, readable style, plus three gauges for progress through the week, quarter, and year.
  • Two-Week Focus — Big week number with a quarter progress gauge and a compact two-row calendar showing the current and next weeks.

Great for

  • Scrum and sprint planning
  • Academic weeks and timetables
  • Project/release cycles and reporting periods

Place it, glance at it, get on with your day.
