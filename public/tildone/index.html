Tildone

Sticky to-do notes for macOS

Keep tasks visible until they are done.

Tildone gives you simple desktop notes for the lists you want in front of you while you work. Check tasks off as you go, minimize notes when you need space, and let finished notes fade away.

  • Create unlimited sticky notes and task lists
  • Adjust colors, transparency, text size, and note visibility
  • Use Focus Filters to blur task text or keep notes quietly in the background
Download for Mac See release log

Why it is useful

Simple notes with just enough workflow

  • Active notes can stay visible by default, so important tasks are harder to forget.
  • Completed notes can fade away, keeping the desktop clear without extra cleanup.
  • Tildone does not collect data, and Focus Filters help reduce distractions when needed.
